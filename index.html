Social Justice Social Engagement Platform

News & Then is a pilot product developed by WGBH Digital funded by the Ford Foundation. We set out to create an engaging destination for a millennial audience. We are connecting today's headlines to historic fights for justice and human rights in the United States, allowing the audience to explore and while watching video clips from American Experience provide their current commentary via Twitter on the issue at hand.
